A good high chair is one of the few baby gear purchases you’ll use every single day, often for years. It needs to be safe, easy to clean, and comfortable enough that your little one actually wants to sit in it. But “high chair” covers everything from full-size convertible towers that last through the preschool years to slim booster seats you can toss in a car for grandma’s house. Before you buy, think about your space, your budget, and how long you want the chair to last. Full-size models with multiple recline and tray positions cost more but grow with your child. Booster seats save floor space and are great for travel, but they don’t always recline or fit very young infants. We looked at owner feedback, expert reviews, and durability reports across the most popular models to help you find the right fit without spending more than you need to.
