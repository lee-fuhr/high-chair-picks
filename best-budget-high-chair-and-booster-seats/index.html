High chairs are one of the most-used gear items in a household with a young child, so finding one that balances safety, ease of cleaning, and cost is essential. Budget models often skip fancy extras like reclining seats or height adjustments, but the best ones still deliver a sturdy, easy-to-wipe seat that keeps your little one secure during meals. The key is choosing a style that fits your space and lifestyle, whether that’s a compact clamp-on chair, a portable booster, or a convertible seat that grows with your child. When shopping on a budget, focus on basics: a stable base (or secure latching system for booster seats), a removable, dishwasher-safe tray, and a seat pad that wipes clean easily. Avoid models with fabric that requires machine washing unless you’re prepared for frequent laundry.
